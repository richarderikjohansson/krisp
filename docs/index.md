## Kiruna Radiometric Inference and Software Platform

This is the documentation the Kiruna Radiometric Inference and Software Platform (KRISP), a software platform designed by scientists for scientists.
KRISP is primarily used for the retrieval of atmospheric trace gases from two microwave radiometers,
KIMRA and MIRA2, both operated at the Swedish Institute of Space Physics in Kiruna, Sweden.

The software is built around the [PyARTS](https://atmtools.github.io/arts-docs-2.6/installation.html) library, a comprehensive toolkit for radiative transfer calculations,
as well as simulation and retrieval applications.

This documentation primarily serves future scientists whom intend to initiate retrievals of KIRMA or MIRA2 data, or general simulations via KRISP's 
[CLI](cli/cli.md), but also future developers and maintainers.
